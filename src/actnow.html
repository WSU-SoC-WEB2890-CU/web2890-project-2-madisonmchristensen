ACT NOW

The mountains are calling and I must can't go.

Along Utah's Famous Fruitway lies the majestic Wasatch Range, and... gravel pits. Five gravel pits within nine short miles. Leaving the cities of Brigham City, Perry, and Willard covered in dust and surrounded by loud semi trucks. Willard recently has become unlucky with Granite Construction's choice to blockade Willard Canyon in order to continue to mine at their gravel pit. This decision as shut off mountain access to the community, meaning no hiking, biking, climbing in the mountains of Willard.

This gravel pit expansions whittles away at the flora and fauna of the mountains we call home. Without canyon access, the citizens of Willard can not fully enjoy what the mountains have to offer.
